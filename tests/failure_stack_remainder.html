Michelle Cardone

Financial services marketer by day, calligrapher/handletter-er/design enthusiast by night. I am a self-taught calligrapher, inspired by my grandfather Dante's beautiful and completely unattainable skill. He was a real artist.

Proficient in InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop, with coursework experience in the Graphic Design program at University of Massachusetts Lowell. Bachelor's degree in Communications Media Studies from Northeastern University.
